What Gets Flagged?

The audit system checks for:

  • Zero totals: Invoices with no amount
  • Missing items: Invoices with no line items
  • Statistical outliers: Amounts significantly different from your history
  • Missing email: Client has no email address for delivery
  • VAT inconsistencies: VAT charged but no VAT number registered
  • Duplicate items: Same item appearing multiple times on invoice

When enabled, flagged invoices appear in your Audit Report for manual review before sending.

Quick Tips
🚫 Turn It Off?

Uncheck "Enable Anomaly Detection" to process all invoices without any audit checks.

🎯 Too Many Flags?

Try a "Lenient" setting to reduce false positives.

🔍 Specific Checks?

Disable individual checks if they don't apply to your workflow.

📊 Review Flagged Invoices

Visit the Audit Report to see flagged invoices and approve them for sending.
