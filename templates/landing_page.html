About Billing v2

Billing v2 is a comprehensive, multi-tenant application designed to help freelancers and small businesses streamline their invoicing and time-tracking processes.

Key Features:
  • Client Management: Keep all your client information organized in one place.
  • Time Tracking: Log hours spent on projects and categorize your work.
  • Invoicing: Generate professional invoices from your timesheet entries with ease.
  • Payment Tracking: Record payments and keep track of outstanding balances.
  • VAT Support: Configure VAT rates and generate VAT reports.
  • Customizable Profiles: Set up your business profile with your company details and logo.

This application is built with Django, a high-level Python web framework, and utilizes Bootstrap 5 for a clean, modern user interface.
